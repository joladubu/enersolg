Diesel Delivery Service

We provide Door To Door delivery services to clients in different spheres of industries at different locations. We are able to achieve this feat using our Diesel Trucks to service needs of customers.

Management of Retail Outlet

In order to maximize the full potential of the retail outlets, we service quite a number of PSP operators, who stop by at the petrol station for purchase of diesel, PMS and DPK products.

Procurement Services

We offer procurement/sourcing services to clients in the Exploration and Production industry. Some of the procurement services we offer are purchase and supply of items, materials and logistics.

Bulk Breaking

We also take up bulk diesel products from the Independent Diesel supply Marketers at the tank farms and sell to those who buy in bulk.
