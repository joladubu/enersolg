about us

Headquartered in Lagos Nigeria, Enersol Global Resources is wholly an indigenous company providing procurement and logistics services of petroleum products supplies (AGO, PMS, LPFO, Offshore Bunker supplies), sourcing and supply of equipment and Haulage and cargo handling amongst several others.

Using our trucks (peddlers) we provide door to door diesel supplies to our clients in the sectors ranging from communication, financial, hospitality, entertainment, residential estates to health sector, government parastatals, manufacturing etc etc.

Since our founding in 2008, Enersol has been engineered to head towards a relentless pursuit for excellence in order to bring satisfaction to both her existing and potential clients with special emphasis and genuine concerns for the operational environment.
