About Enersol

Enersol Global Resources was initially founded in 2008 by 2 passionate business leaders with a combined sales & distribution experience in the downstream sector of more than 18 years in Nigeria. Our ambition is to become a leading distribution & fulfilment partner that “goes the extra mile”, offering added value services beyond simple transportation from a to b – such as retail strategy consultancy and creative tactical Sales & Marketing support. We always take a personal approach and ultimately aim becoming your success story. We offer tailored, flexible and cost effective solutions and continuously invest into improving our state of the art systems and processes to support automation and scalability.

The nature of our business sees us providing Procurement and Logistics services such as Petroleum products supplies (AGO, PMS, LPFO, Offshore Bunker supplies), Sourcing and supply of equipment and Haulage and cargo handling amongst several others. We also specialize in managing retail petrol/gas outlets where we dispense our products (diesel, petrol, Kerosene etc) to the public.
